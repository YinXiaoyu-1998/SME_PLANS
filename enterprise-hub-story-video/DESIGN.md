# 企业资料中枢视频视觉规范

## Style Prompt

1920x1080 横版科技感产品演示视频。画面像高端 AI 工作台和企业数据指挥舱：深黑蓝背景、玻璃拟态面板、细网格、数据流线、发光节点、清晰中文大标题。整体克制、可信、企业级，不做夸张赛博朋克。重点突出“员工手中的通用 AI agent”和“企业资料中枢”之间的自然协作。

## Colors

- Background: `#061018`
- Panel: `#0B1724`
- Panel Glass: `rgba(13, 31, 47, 0.78)`
- Primary Cyan: `#38D8FF`
- Accent Amber: `#F7B955`
- Success Green: `#4CE0A8`
- Text Main: `#F4F8FB`
- Text Muted: `#9FB3C8`
- Danger Red: `#FF6B7A`

## Typography

- Chinese / UI: `Noto Sans SC`, `Inter`, sans-serif
- Code / terminal: `JetBrains Mono`, monospace
- Headlines use heavy weight, large but not decorative.

## Motion

- Smooth interface motion: slide, reveal, pulse, data-line sweep.
- No chaotic spinning. Use precise, confident movement.
- Use wipe/glass scan transitions between scenes.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use colorful rainbow gradients.
- Do not make the product look like a crypto dashboard.
- Do not show database or server implementation details.
- Do not overuse tiny unreadable text.
- Do not imply the资料中枢 itself runs analysis or generates reports.
